Thank You to Our Generous Sponsors

The Matsila Foundation is proud to work with organizations and individuals who share our vision of supporting communities in need. Their contributions make our work possible.

Major Sponsors

  • ABC Supermarkets – Donations of non-perishable food items.
  • WarmHearts Clothing – Clothing and blankets for winter drives.
  • Ubuntu Bank – Financial support for our feeding schemes.
  • Local Community Churches – Volunteers and outreach programs.

Individual Donors

Beyond our major sponsors, the Matsila Foundation relies heavily on the generosity of countless individual donors. These dedicated supporters contribute in various ways, whether through monthly donations, one-time gifts, or volunteering their time and skills. Each contribution, no matter the size, plays a crucial role in sustaining our programs and expanding our reach to more communities in need. By supporting our initiatives, individual donors become active partners in our mission to fight hunger, provide clothing and blankets, and foster educational opportunities. Their commitment allows us to plan long-term projects, respond swiftly to urgent needs, and maintain a consistent presence in the communities we serve. The impact of these contributions extends far beyond material resources; they embody a shared vision of compassion, community, and social responsibility, ensuring that our work continues to transform lives and uplift families throughout South Africa.
